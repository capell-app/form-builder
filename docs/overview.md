# Form Builder

<!-- prettier-ignore-start -->

## What it does for you

Form Builder lets you build forms, put them on a page, and collect what people submit. You drag in fields, set which are required, embed the form, read every submission in one place, and get an email when someone fills it in.

## Your screens

- **Forms**: your forms and where they are used.
- **The form editor**: where you add and arrange fields.
- **Submissions**: everything people have submitted.

## What you can do

- Build a form by adding fields.
- Make fields required and validate them.
- Embed a form on a page.
- View submissions.
- Get notified when a form is filled in.

## Where to find it

Go to **Form Builder** in the admin to build forms and read submissions.

## Good to know

- Drag fields to build your form, and mark the ones people must fill in.
- See every **submission** in one place.
- Get a **notification email** when someone fills it in.
